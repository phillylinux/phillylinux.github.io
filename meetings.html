PLUG Central meetings are held on the first Wednesday of the month at the University of the Sciences in Philadelphia

PLUG North meetings are held on the second Tuesday of the month at the offices of Coredial in Blue Bell.

PLUG West meetings are currently on hiatus while we look for a new venue.
Many thanks to ATS for hosting PLUG West from 2015-2019.

All meetings start at 7:00 P.M.
Click the location name for location-specific information,
such as driving and public transportation directions.

If there is a subject on which you would like to give about
a one hour presentation, please email Paul Snyder or Walt Mankowski.

Cost: FREE - Everyone with an interest in Linux is welcome to attend
RSVP: Except for special circumstances (which will be announced), no RSVPs are required. Everyone is encouraged to just show up.
Agenda:
7:00pm - 8:00pm   Q&A
8:00pm - 9:00pm   Presentation

We strongly recommend that you subscribe to at least the very low
traffic announce list so that you can be informed of any changes.
The discussion list also receives these announcements;
there is no need to be subscribed to both.
